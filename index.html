Worlds to Build

Wonderfilled, Inc. is a transmedia studio and publisher founded by Stephen Erin Dinehart IV — the designer who coined the term Narrative Designer in 2006 and helped define the discipline now standard across the industry.

From AAA titles to Super Nintendo World, from GiantLands to Emperor of the Cherokee — we build worlds with depth, intention, and longevity. Our properties are designed to transcend platform: games, novels, experiences, and theme parks that share one mythology.

We operate at the frontier where ancient story meets emergent technology — and we believe that the studios of the future are also the guardians of myth.
