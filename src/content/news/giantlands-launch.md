After years of development, the **GiantLands** transmedia universe made its public debut in November 2025 with three simultaneous releases — the broadest launch of any tabletop RPG system in the studio's history.

## The Releases

**Tuskaloosa's Reckoning** is the core adventure module — the entry point into the GiantLands universe. Players take on the roles of explorers navigating the Fifth Age, a world shaped by ancestral American myth where the giants who built the mounds have faded into legend, but their works still define the landscape. The adventure is structured for both one-shots and extended campaigns.

**The Broken Road** is a campaign sourcebook expanding the geographic and political landscape of the Fifth Age. It details the trade routes, faction conflicts, and mythological fault lines that define the world between settlements — and provides the Game Master with the tools to run extended play in the GiantLands universe.

**Fifth Age Codex** is the world bible — the complete reference document for the GiantLands mythology. Covering cosmology, history, peoples, creatures, sacred sites, and the deep lore of the ancestral American myth tradition that underpins the setting. An essential companion for players and designers alike.

## The Universe

GiantLands has been in development as a long-form transmedia universe for over a decade. The tabletop RPG is the first publicly available layer of a mythology that will eventually span novels, adventure modules, themed experiences, and a landmark installation concept at Aztalan State Park in Wisconsin.

All three titles are available now through the Wonderfilled catalog.
