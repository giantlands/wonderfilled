Stephen Erin Dinehart IV's essay **"The Spaghetti Strategy"** was published in *IEEE Computer*, the flagship publication of the IEEE Computer Society, in 2025.

The piece addresses a fundamental challenge in AI-assisted narrative design: how to manage the combinatorial explosion of emergent story possibilities that generative systems produce, without either over-constraining the output or losing coherent narrative shape.

The essay introduces the "spaghetti strategy" as a design philosophy — embracing the messy, tangled nature of emergent narrative as a feature rather than a bug, and providing a framework for designers to work productively within it rather than against it.

*IEEE Computer* is one of the oldest and most respected peer-reviewed publications in the computing field. Publication in the magazine represents recognition of Dinehart's work at the intersection of narrative design and computational systems — a body of research that spans his academic career and directly informs Wonderfilled's approach to AI and emerging media.

The full text is available to IEEE members and through institutional subscriptions.
